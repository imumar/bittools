Loan EMI Calculator

Compute EMI, total interest and total payment.

How to use
  1. Enter principal, annual interest %, and tenure in months.
  2. Click Calculate to see EMI, total interest, and total payment.
About EMI

EMI uses a fixed-rate amortization formula with monthly compounding.

Tips

  • Lower rates or longer tenure reduce EMI but increase total interest.
  • Prepayments reduce outstanding principal and future interest.
