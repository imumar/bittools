Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMR?

BMR is the calories your body burns at rest to maintain vital functions.

What is TDEE?

TDEE estimates total daily calories based on activity level and BMR.

How do you calculate BMR?

Use formulas based on gender, weight, height, and age as shown above.

How do you calculate TDEE?

Multiply BMR by an activity factor: Sedentary:1.2, Light:1.375, Moderate:1.55, Very:1.725, Super Active:1.9
