What This Word Counter Measures

                
    
                    
  • Words — detected by whitespace boundaries.
    • 
                    
  • Characters — with and without spaces.
    • 
                    
  • Lines — based on line breaks.
    • 
                    
  • Sentences — estimated using punctuation like ., !, ?.
    • 
                    
  • Paragraphs — separated by blank lines.
    • 
                    
  • Reading Time — based on ~200 WPM.
    • 
                    
  • Speaking Time — based on ~130 WPM.

Frequently Asked Questions

                    structures.


                
🔹 Is there a character count without spaces?

                
Yes — the output shows both total characters and characters excluding spaces.
