Frequently Asked Questions

                
🔹 What does "Remove Line Breaks" do?

                
It replaces line breaks with a single space so lines are joined into one continuous line.


                
🔹 What does "Remove Extra Spaces" do?

                
It collapses sequences of multiple whitespace characters (spaces, tabs) into a single space and trims
                    leading/trailing spaces on lines.


                
🔹 What does "Remove Both" do?

                
It first removes line breaks (joining lines), then collapses extra spaces — producing a clean
                    single-line result.
