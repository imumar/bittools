Case Conversion Explained

                

                    UPPERCASE converts all alphabetic characters to capital letters.

                    lowercase converts all alphabetic characters to small letters.

                    Title Case capitalizes the first letter of principal words (common words like
                    “and”, “the”, “of” are left lowercase unless they start the sentence).

Frequently Asked Questions

                
🔹 Does Title Case handle small words (a, the, of)?

                
Yes — Title Case implementation here uses a conservative rule: it capitalizes main words and keeps
                    common short words lowercase unless they appear at the start of a line or sentence.


                
🔹 Will punctuation be changed?

                
No — punctuation and special characters are preserved exactly as in the input text.


                
🔹 Is this tool free and private?

                
Yes. BitTools offers this tool for free and it runs completely in your browser, so your text is never
