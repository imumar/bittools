Our Story

BitTools started as a personal project to solve common development challenges. As developers ourselves, we often found ourselves needing quick, reliable tools for everyday tasks like formatting JSON, testing regular expressions, or decoding JWT tokens. However, many existing tools were either too complex, required installation, or compromised on privacy.

What began as a collection of simple utilities has grown into a comprehensive suite of development and productivity tools, all while maintaining our core principles of simplicity, privacy, and accessibility.
